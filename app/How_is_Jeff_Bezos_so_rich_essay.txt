Jeff Bezos, the founder of Amazon, is one of the wealthiest individuals in the world, and his fortunes can be attributed to a combination of innovative business strategies, astute investments, and the growth trajectory of technology. As of 2024, Bezos owns approximately 10.8% of Amazon, which remains his primary source of wealth. This essay explores how Bezos accumulated his massive fortune through his leadership at Amazon, his various investments, and his ventures outside the e-commerce giant.

At the heart of Jeff Bezos's wealth is Amazon, the company he founded in 1994 as an online bookstore. Over the years, Bezos transformed it into a colossal multinational tech company, leading the charge in areas such as cloud computing, online retail, streaming, and artificial intelligence. With Amazon dominating the e-commerce landscape, Bezos's stake in the company has allowed him to amass significant wealth.
